The Public Information Officer (PIO) manages the content, execution and implementation of the Borough's digital and print communications including its website, calendar, newsletter, social media, mailers and special event coordination and promotion as well as public relations.

The PIO also assists the Governing Body in their efforts to collect and disseminate information and facilitates day-to-day communications between the Governing Body and the community and any projects they may have. Additionally, the PIO manages the Office of the Mayor as well as the Board, Commission and Committee application and interview process.
