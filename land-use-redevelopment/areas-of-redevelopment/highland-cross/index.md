# Highland Cross Warehouse/Light Industrial Development

![Map of Highland Cross](highlandmap.jpg){: .img-fluid }

**Location:** The end of Highland Cross, east of Route 17 and located primarily within the zoning authority of the NJSEA.

**Project Description:** 

路 A 357,521 square foot warehouse/light industrial facility

路 8 Loading docks

路 206 exterior & 175 interior van parking spaces

路 Construction is underway
